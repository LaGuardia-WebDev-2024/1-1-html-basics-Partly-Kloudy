It Lingers

On a cold, dark, misty kind of day,
As a ray of sun shines through,
Hope, it lingers in the air,
Like the smell of morning rain.

Love, it lingers in my heart,
As I see you in my dreams!
We would never fall apart,
This is love to me it seems!

On a warm summer morning,
The sky is clear.
The sun is shining,
As I hold you near.

I’ll walk across a river
To get to you.
Grab my hand!
We’ll make it through.

I imagine sun,
I imagine love,
I’m calling out to you.

I live in my dreams
Falling apart at the seams
I imagine someone!

Lonely walking through the street,
I will think of you some days.
You linger in my mind,
Yes I know this isn’t real.
