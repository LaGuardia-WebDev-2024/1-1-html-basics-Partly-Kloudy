Examples of Natural Disasters

Natural disasters are weather events that happen from Earth's Natural processes.

Hurricanes

Tropical cyclones that usually occur over warm seawaters. They start when the warm seawater evaporates quickly, creating a storm.

Tornados

A weather event that includes high speed winds. There are different level tornados.

Earthquakes

Occur when the tectonic plates of the Earth shift. Usually causes volcanic eruptions and used to create mountains.
